# Specification: UAE Automotive Regulations Guide

## Overview
This track aims to create a definitive technical resource for UAE automotive regulations at `/hub/regulations`. By providing actionable guidance on compliance, we establish Smart Motor as a trusted expert for luxury car owners while driving bookings for tinting, exhaust repairs, and pre-RTA inspections.

## Functional Requirements

### 1. Regulatory Intelligence Modules
- **Window Tinting:** Technical breakdown of VLT (Visible Light Transmission) limits (e.g., 50% for standard, special permissions).
- **Modified Exhausts:** Guidance on decibel limits, emission standards, and the MOI certificate process.
- **RTA Passing Standards:** Key technical check-points (tires, oil leaks, lights, emissions) for successful registration.
- **Aesthetic Mods:** Rules regarding color changes, body kits, and structural modifications.

### 2. Authority UI/UX
- **Tone:** Technical and Actionable ("The Smart Way to Stay Legal").
- **Visuals:** High-fidelity "Engineering Matrix" aesthetic.
- **Components:** Interactive regulation cards, "Legal Limit" badges, and technical diagrams where applicable.

### 3. Service Funnels
- **Linking:** Contextual links to XPEL (tinting), Akrapovič (exhaust), and standard mechanical services.
- **CTA:** "Book Pre-RTA Technical Audit" as a primary conversion point.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] New route `/hub/regulations` is live and SEO-optimized.
- [ ] Content includes all four prioritized regulatory areas.
- [ ] Links to "Precision Parts" and booking forms are integrated.
- [ ] Mobile-first responsive grid implemented.

## Out of Scope
- Legal representation or fine processing.
- Direct interaction with RTA/MOI systems.
