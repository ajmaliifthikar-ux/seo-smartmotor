Do

10. Universal Icon Library

Paginated view of all Car Brand, Tech, and Automotive icons.

Logo Intro Animation

A high-energy, transparent logo sting for use in video content and mobile app splash screens.

Download Original (.mov)

Do

  • Use this animation as a quick intro/outro for social media videos.
  • Ensure the video is set to `autoplay`, `muted`, `loop`, and `playsinline` when used on the web.

Don't

  • For production websites, do not use the raw `.mov` file. It must be converted to an optimized, web-compatible format like `.webm` or a Lottie `.json` file.
  • Do not play with sound unless explicitly required, as it can be intrusive on auto-playing social media feeds.
