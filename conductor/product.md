# Product Definition: Smart Motor Platform

## Vision
A 24/7 digital concierge that books services, answers questions, runs marketing, and briefs the owner on business performance — so the garage can focus on fixing cars.

## Target Audience
1.  **Customer (Car Owner):** Owns a luxury vehicle (AED 200K–2M+). Values time above money. Expects premium treatment.
2.  **Service Advisor:** Non-technical. Needs information at a glance.
3.  **Owner / GM:** Business-minded. Wants actionable intelligence.
4.  **Marketing Lead:** Needs leverage to produce content.

## Three Laws of Smart Motor
1.  **Law of Elimination:** Does this remove a step, a wait, or a confusion?
2.  **Law of Luxury:** Would this feel at home on a Rolls-Royce dashboard?
3.  **Law of Autonomy:** Can the AI handle this without a human?

## Core Features
1.  **AI Concierge (Leyla):** Replaces the receptionist for off-hours and assists during business hours.
2.  **Booking Engine:** 4-screen flow, <60 seconds, real-time availability.
3.  **Customer Portal:** Personalized space for car owners to track bookings, manage their fleet, and view loyalty status.
4.  **Admin OS:** 6 departments (Dashboard, Basecamp, CMS, Studio, Strategy Lab, Workbench).
5.  **Strategy Lab:** Autonomous AI swarm for real-time market reconnaissance, SEO self-optimization, and PhD-level strategic planning.
